Top 5 Marksmen in Arena of Valor

Valhein

Valhein is a versatile marksman with a mix of magic and physical damage.

Violet

Violet excels in mobility and burst damage, making her a top pick in many games.

Yorn

Yorn's continuous damage output and long-range attacks make him a formidable foe.

Fennik

Fennik is known for his high attack speed and ability to deal damage over time.

Slimz

Slimz is a marksman with a strong early game and excellent range.

Why These Marksmen Are Top Tier

  • High Damage Output
  • Excellent Range
  • Strong Team Fight Presence
